Shaun the Sheep's Pizza

Description

A delicious pizza recipe inspired by Shaun the Sheep's love for pizza. This recipe is perfect for a hearty meal and is sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1 lb pizza sauce
  • 1 lb mozzarella cheese
  • 1 lb pepperoni slices

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Shred the mozzarella cheese and sprinkle it over the sauce.
  5. Arrange the pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
